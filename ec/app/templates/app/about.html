Our Journey

A Story of Passion, Quality & Community

Fresh Dairy is a public limited company with registered office at Pokhara City of Gandaki State in Nepal. Established in 2018 and beginning production in 2020, we've quickly become one of the fastest-growing dairy companies in the region.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and ethical farming practices has earned us the trust of thousands of customers across Nepal. We believe in creating a sustainable future for farmers and communities while delivering premium dairy products to your doorstep.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

The guiding principles that drive everything we do

Our Mission

To embrace technology and innovation to delight our customers with premium quality dairy products. We are committed to empowering the farmer community through our unique 'Relationship Farming' model, ensuring fair prices and sustainable practices.

  • Deliver high-quality dairy products
  • Support farmer livelihoods
  • Embrace innovative technologies

Our Vision

To become Nepal's most trusted and sustainable dairy brand, known for quality, innovation, and social responsibility. We envision a future where every family enjoys pure, nutritious dairy products while supporting ethical farming practices.

  • Lead the dairy industry in Nepal
  • Promote sustainable agriculture
  • Build lasting community relationships
