## 🎨 Customization Tips

### Change Green to Any Color
Find in `style.css`:
```css
--brand-900: #0a1f1b;  ← Change primary
--brand-700: #1a5c52;  ← Change secondary
--brand-500: #2faa6b;  ← Change accent
```

### Adjust Animation Speed
Change all `0.3s` to `0.2s` for faster or `0.5s` for slower

### Modify Hover Effects
Adjust `transform: translateY(-16px)` to change lift distance

### Change Button Padding
Update `padding: 14px 42px` to different values
